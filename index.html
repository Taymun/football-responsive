Organisation

The CONMEBOL Copa América 2021 in Brazil will be the 47th edition of the tournament, the main football competition between the South American national teams, organized by the South American Football Confederation (CONMEBOL). It will begin its new edition on June 13, 2021 at Mané Garrincha Stadium, Brasília, and will crown a new continental champion at Maracanã, in Rio de Janeiro, on July 10.
